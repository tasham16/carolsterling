About Me

Who I am...

Hi, I'm Carol! I'm an arts educator and teaching artist and I've spent over 50 years dedicating my life's work as an advocate for the importance of arts education in the lives of people across all ages. In that time, I have had the opportunity to serve in arts education and leadership positions on local, state, regional, national and international levels.

What I do...

As a consultant, I guide teachers, administrators, curriculum coordinators, after school programs, arts in education programs, arts councils, senior centers, libraries, - anyone designing, developing and evaluating arts programs for all ages!

Additionally, I have developed expertise as an educational puppeteer. I work with pre-service and in-service teachers, undergraduate and graduate students in Education, sharing strategies on how to integrate puppetry to enrich the language arts, social studies, health/nutrition/wellness, and science curricula in grades Pre-K-12.
