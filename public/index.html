HI, I'M CAROL STERLING!

I'm

I am an arts educator and teaching artist who has devoted my life's work to advocate for the importance of arts education in the lives of people all ages, from young people to older adults, and have served in arts and education leadership positions on local, state, regional, national and international levels for over fifty years.
